--- layout: travel title: Travel --- I want to keep this list as short and easy to remember as possible. It should allow constructing simple sentences (with incorrect but mostly understandable grammar) without sounding rude or like an idiot. For some languages, i intentionally left out some expressions if they are not frequently used (for example, "good afternoon" in several languages).

I would be grateful if you can contribute to this list. But please, only do so if you know the language well. I am not looking for a direct translation of all the words (i can get that from online translators). I am interested in words and their grammatical forms that are most useful for constructing understandable sentences (even if the grammar is not correct). If there are multiple ways of translating a word, tell me the most useful ones. If your language has a very common expression that is not common in English, let me know.

SVO = Subject-Verb-Object sentence structure.
