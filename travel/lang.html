--- layout: travel title: Travel --- Here is my list of most useful words and expressions in their most common grammatical form. These words listed here should be sufficient for making simple sentences (with incorrect but mostly understandable grammar). The idea for this list is to make it as short as possible, allowing basic communication (supported with hand gestures) without sounding rude or like a total idiot. For some languages, i intentionally left out expressions that are not frequently used (to keep the list simple).

I would be very grateful if you can contribute to this list. But please, only do so if you know the language well. I am not interested in a direct translation of all the words (i can get that from online translators). I am interested in words and their grammatical forms that are most useful for constructing understandable sentences (even if the grammar is not correct). If there are multiple ways of translating a word, tell me the most useful one. If your language has a very common expression that is not common in English, let me know.

SVO = Subject-Verb-Object sentence structure.
