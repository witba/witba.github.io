PREPARATION

Essentials:
What are the entry requirements (visas, permits, vaccinations...)
Is the country/region safe to visit? (heck tavel advisory pages like ... if not sure)
What is the current situation (holidays, elections, unrest)
What weather can you expect? Will you be at a high altitude?
If going to multiple countries, how will you cross the borders? Are land crossings open to tourists? Are the flights expensive?
How will you handle money? Can you use local ATMs?
