PREPARATION

Essentials:
  • What are the entry requirements (visas, permits, vaccinations...)
  • Is the country/region safe to visit? (check news and tavel advisory pages like this one if not sure)
  • What is the current situation (holidays, elections, unrest)
  • What weather should you expect? Will you be at a high altitude?
  • If going to multiple countries, how will you cross the borders? Are land crossings open to tourists? Are the flights expensive?
  • How will you handle money? Can you use local ATMs?

    Also:
  • Write down important information (bank card numbers, passwords, plane tickets) using a secret code that only you can solve. Email it to yourself, along with scans of important documents.
  • Sometimes it is worth to carry a printed copy of your passport.
