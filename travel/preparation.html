PREPARATION

Essentials:
What are the entry requirements (visas, permits, vaccinations...)
Is the country/region safe to visit? (check tavel advisory pages like this one if not sure)
What is the current situation (holidays, elections, unrest)
What weather can you expect? Will you be at a high altitude?
If going to multiple countries, how will you cross the borders? Are land crossings open to tourists? Are the flights expensive?
How will you handle money? Can you use local ATMs?

Also:
Write down important information (bank card numbers, telephone numbers, plane tickets) using a secret code that only you can solve. Email it to yourself, along with scans of your documents (passport, insurance...).
Sometimes it is worth to carry a printed copy of your passport.
