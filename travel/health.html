Altitude sickness:

More dangerous that most people imagine. Even if you don't develop severe symptoms it can do permanent damage to your brain and you won't even know about it. At worst, it can kill you. Be careful when going above 3000 m and give yourself a few days to get acclimatized.

Don't neglect minor cuts on your skin, especially in places with poor hygiene. Make sure they heal properly.
