Altitude sickness:
Even if you don't notice severe symptoms it can do permanent damage to your brain. At worst, it can kill you. Be careful when going above 3000 m and give yourself a few days to get acclimatized.

Don't neglect minor cuts on your skin, especially in places with poor hygiene. Make sure they heal properly.
