Useful resources:
  • Wikitravel Free online guide
  • Thorn Tree forum Travel related questions/answers
  • Travelpod Travelpod blogs were invaluable for finding information about obscure places. Unfortunately, it got bought and later shut down by Tripadvisor. Please boycott Tripadvisor.
  • Summitpost For hiking and climbing
  • Climate Maps Climate maps for each month
  • Rome2rio Find bus, train, and ferry connections
  • UNESCO World Heritage Sites
  • World Heritage Sites map A more detailed map of UNESCO sites
  • Sightsmap Use it if you don't have time to do proper research about a place but want to know where to go
  • Opentopomap Topographic maps
  • Hikebikemap Maps of hiking trails
  • Openbusmap Maps of bus and train routes
  • Berkeley Earth's Air Quality map Air pollution is a leading killer
  • UK's travel advice: Look at maps of high risk countries and areas
  • France's travel advice: If you don't know French, just see the maps near the bottom of the page
  • Visa requirements on Wikipedia See your country's page for a list of countries you need a visa for
  • Flightconnections Great for planning flight routes
